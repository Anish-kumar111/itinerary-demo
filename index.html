Do I pay for affiliate bookings?
No. Hotels, flights, and deals are booked at the partner's standard (or member) prices — Tripster earns a commission from the partner, not you.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel on your own — changes apply immediately, and we prorate your bill fairly.
What does Crew actually include?
All Pro features, for up to 6 members on one bill, plus unlimited group co-editing, a shared wallet for splitting costs, and human travel concierge.
Do you offer student discounts?
Yes — 50% off Pro with a valid student email (.edu, .ac.uk, .ac.in, .edu.au, and more). Verify your student ID and the discount applies automatically.
Is my data safe?
We never sell your data. Partner accounts use OAuth — we don't see your passwords. Delete your data in one tap from Settings → Privacy.
